# Governance / 治理

## Constitutional purpose
Transform DIKWP and artificial-consciousness research assets into citable, reproducible, executable and sustainably maintained public products.

## Powers
- The runtime may calculate vitality and generate `EvolutionProposal` objects.
- It may not merge its own proposals, modify the Purpose Constitution, create production credentials or impersonate a natural person.
- High-impact changes require two maintainers, one external reviewer and a reproducible before/after report.

## Stewardship succession
If the origin repository has no signed maintainer release for 180 days, registered mirror stewards may publish a continuity fork. The fork must preserve lineage, use a distinct identifier and must not claim to be the original natural person.

## Metrics are not truth
Downloads, page views and stars are low-weight energy signals. Independent reproduction, contradictions, citations, merged repairs and non-founder releases carry higher weight. No single score is a scientific validity certificate.
